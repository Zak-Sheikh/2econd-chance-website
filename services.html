Everything you need to get stable and stay housed, with 1:1 support every step of the way.

Transitioning Services

For individuals and families without stable housing, we guide you through the steps to secure safe and affordable shelter. Our dedicated housing specialists meet you where you‚Äôre at‚Äîoffering hands-on support to overcome application barriers and housing access challenges.

  • Assistance applying for Section 8, GRH, Project-Based, or other subsidized housing
  • Help securing vital documents (ID, income verification, references)
  • Transportation coordination for housing tours and lease signings
  • Direct advocacy with property managers and leasing offices
  • In-person support on move-in day to ensure a smooth transition

Sustaining Services

Housing is just the beginning. Our sustaining services are designed to keep you housed long-term by building trust, stability, and resilience through consistent support and resource access.

  • Ongoing check-ins to identify and address housing risks before they escalate
  • Help managing rent responsibilities, communication with landlords, and lease renewals
  • Connection to employment services, health resources, legal aid, and education programs
  • Crisis planning (eviction prevention, emergency relocation, mental health support)
  • Celebrating stability milestones together and setting long-term goals
